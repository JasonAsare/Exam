Hello I am Jason
A Software Developer

I’m Jason Omanye Asare, a 13-year-old in grade 8, and I’ve got a big passion for coding, especially web development. I love building websites using HTML and CSS, and I’m always looking for new ways to improve my skills and try out different ideas. Coding is something I find both fun and challenging, and I’m excited to learn more as I get older. I’m also very curious about how technology works, and I can’t wait to dive deeper into more advanced languages in the future. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me on the basketball court, playing with friends, or diving into games like Roblox and Minecraft. I love the creativity and adventure these games offer, and they give me a fun break from my tech projects. Whether I’m shooting hoops or building virtual worlds, I try to balance all my hobbies and passions, enjoying both the digital and physical worlds as I grow.
